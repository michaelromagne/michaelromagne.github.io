Combat fraudsters at Ubisoft

Limit frauds for online merchants.

Duration: 5 min | Date: Sept 22, 2023

For the last 3 years, I have mostly been working on a Fraud Detection use case at Ubisoft. The objective of the project is to analyze in-game transactions on PC in real time, and block the ones that are too risky. Which risk are we talking about ? In short, big chargeback fees, a lower acceptance rate and a bad reputation.

The risk of Fraud

Bank chargebacks : fees and exponential growth

How do we tackle it ?

Components : Platform hosted on AWS, Feature store with spark flows, and a ML model (xgboost).
